RULES

NORMAL MODE

  • You have to move the blue ball using left and right arrow keys and escape from the spike on the top.
  • Your ball would be initially kept on a slab , once you move out of the slab , your ball would free fall.
  • You should prevent you ball to neither hit on the spike on top of screen , nor fall down below the screen
  • Collect Health Pickups to increase you life !
